Privacy Policy for Renters App

Last Updated: 29th January 2024

Renters is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users' sensitive information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal data from users of the Renters app. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information Collected:

We may collect personal information such as names, contact details, property preferences, and service requests.

2. Usage of Information:

Your information is used to facilitate real estate transactions, connect users with service providers, and enhance user experience.

3. Data Security:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our commitment to data security extends to all stages of data handling.

4. Data Sharing:

Renters may share your information with third-party service providers, as necessary for real estate transactions and service fulfillment. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy:

Renters does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Your Choices:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can update your preferences in the app's settings or contact our support team for assistance.

7. Changes to Policy:

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We recommend reviewing it to stay informed about how we protect your data.

8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at therenters.pk@gmail.com.

Thank you for trusting Renters with your information. We are dedicated to providing a secure and transparent platform for all our users.
